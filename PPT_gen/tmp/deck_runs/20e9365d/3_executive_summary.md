### 2. Client & Project Overview
* **Client / Owner**: City of Dallas
* **Project Name / Title**: Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas (KBHCCD) Master Plan – Component 1
* **Project Type & Use**: Convention Center Expansion
* **Location**: Dallas, Texas
* **High-level Project Intent / Vision**: To expand the KBHCCD to enhance exhibition and meeting space, improve connectivity, and support local economic development.
* **Estimated Construction Budget / Project Cost**: Not specified.

### 3. Scope of Services
* **Project Phases**:
  - Preliminary through Schematic Design
  - Design Development through Constructability Reviews
  - Construction Planning, QA/QC, RFI/IFC Process, Design Validation, Construction Documents
  - Onsite and Construction Administration
* **Consultant Disciplines Required**:
  - Architecture
  - Structural Engineering
  - Civil Engineering
  - MEP Engineering
  - Landscape Architecture
  - Acoustics
  - Security/Safety Consulting
* **Key Technical Requirements**: LEED certification, constructability reviews, and quality assurance processes.
* **Sustainability / Resilience Requirements**: LEED Gold Certification, 90% reduction in fossil fuel consumption.
* **Special Services**: Public engagement, design sequencing, and construction administration.
